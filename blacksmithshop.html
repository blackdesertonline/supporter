Discover what you can buy from the blacksmiths.


The Blacksmiths of Major Towns

The blacksmiths across the world of black desert have helped many of the famous and powerful adventurers we know today. As a new adventurer I would like you to meet them and what they can offer you.

Each of them have one thing in common. They can sell you these very important items.

1. Blacksmith's Secret Book. It is a book that comes with 20, 30, 40, 50 variant and each of these book can store your character's failstack and turn it into . Just remember that you can no longer use it if your fs is higher than the book ex. Blacksmith's Secret Book 50 can only store 50 or below failstack.

2. Cron Stone. Blacksmith also sell cron stones although it's a little bit expensive.

3. Restoration Stone. The item that restores upgraded yellow grade green gears back to green grade gears using your black spirit.

They also have items that are unique to their shop. They can be very convenient with a little increase in price, if you are not able to buy the items in central market due to high demands I recommend you buy from their shop.

Tranan Underfoe of Velia

1. Reblath Gear. This white gear is very famous among adventurers in making failstack from 1 to 25. It is only available in Velia that is why I highly suggest you do enhancing in this town.

2. Luck Armor of Fortuna Gear. A good starter armor that gives movement speed +3.

3. Agerian Gear. A gear that gives stun, stiff, freezing resistance +15% and it is a good starting gear for Shai.

Techthon of Heidel

1. Strength Helmet of Heve Gear. This armor is widely used by veteran adventurers. Some might say that this armor is the best green gear set available.

Dobart of Calpheon

1. Zereth Gear. An armor gear set that gives extra stamina and knockdown/bound resistance +15%. It has only 1 crystal socket per part.

2. Talis Gear. An armor gear set that gives +2 movement speed, jump up and grapple resistance +15%.

Hughol of Duvencrune

1. Mirror of Equilibrium. This has the same function as restoration stone except you use it to extract garmoth's heart in your sub-weapon.

Mevo Muranan

1. Grunil Armor Gear. Many new adventurers can't go wrong with this armor set it. It gives AP and health which every class needs.

Other

Tulem the Arms Dealer in Altinova also offers all class weapons that can also be an alternative if you are not able to buy any from central market.

I hope this simple guide helps you make your own decisions in the future.
