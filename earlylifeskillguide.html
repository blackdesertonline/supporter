This guide will help you decide what type of life skiller you can be as you take your first step towards life skilling.


Life Skills

There are 10 life skill professions in Black Desert they are Gathering, Processing, Fishing, Hunting, Cooking, Alchemy, Horse Training, Trading, Farming and lastly Sailing. Now I firmly believe that there are 3 types of life skiller in black desert they are Full-time, Part-time and Smart Life Skiller which will determine the profession that you should be doing.

The Full Time Life Skiller

Full-time life skillers are the players you see with many laurels on top of their heads. This simply means that they are the top life skillers in game. Their characteristics are as follows :

1. They rarely or don't grind at all.

2. Their main source of income comes from life skilling alone.

3. They are usually AFK and waiting for their energy to get full.

4. They are online 24 hours 7 days a week.

Being a Full-Time Life Skiller requires you to:

1. Buy Value Pack every month.

2. Buy Blessing of Kamasylve.

3. Buy Book of Life.

4. Increase your Contribution Points and Energy.

5. Enhance life skill gears such as Loggia or Manos.

6. Buy the life skill outfits in Pearl Shop.

It takes a lot of hardwork and dedication to be a full time life skiller. They play a very important role in BDO and for that we thank you.

Part Time Life Skiller

They are also known as casual life skillers. They don't earn as much as a full time life skiller would but they benefit from the passive income of nodes. Their characteristics are as follows :

1. Their main source of income comes from casual life skilling and heavy grinding.

2. They life skill only when they want to take a break from hours of grinding and if their energy is full.

3. They usually go with AFK life skill such as Training and Fishing.

Being a Part-time Life Skiller requires you to:

1. Choose 1 or more life skill profession where you can earn silver with minimal to no effort. I would recommend Gathering, Fishing, Farming and Horse Training.

Smart Life Skiller

Smart life skillers are heavy grinders that only do life skill solely to support their personal grinding/pvp needs such as feeding their agile pets, restoring their alchemy stone's durability, combat buffs like Beast (PVE) and Giant (PVP) Draught etc... Their characteristics are as follows :

1. They make their own personal food buffs, pet feed etc...

2. They don't earn silver from life skill.

3. They save considerable amount of silver by life skilling.

Being a Smart Life Skiller requires you to:

1. Build a node empire that specifically targets nodes that will aid your grinding/ combat needs.

2. Increase your contribution points up to 250 or higher.

I hope this guide can help you make your own decisions when it comes to Life Skills.
